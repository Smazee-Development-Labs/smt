What We do ?
We operate on Govt sponsored model as well as on Paid up model courses. Courses will be conducted based on the demand in the Industry. Equipped with Industries best Qualified trainers for each job role. (Regular/ Nonregular) Equipped with better Infrastructure, library and practical training materials. Located in prominent rural places of Tamil Nadu, provides easy access to the needy students. For government funded/aided programs, we always focus to reach the benefits to the rural/ down the level people in the society and make sure of Society development with the Skill Development. Students will be imparted for OJT training with Local relevant Industry Players. Training programs are always linked with Placements
